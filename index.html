The direct detection of radiation originating in exoplanetary atmospheres is a requirement for the identification of spectral signatures indicative of extraterrestrial biological activity. I use the latest generation of ground- and space-based technologies to lead innovative projects that push the envelope in imaging and characterizing exoplanetary systems.
